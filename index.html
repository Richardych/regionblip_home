RegionCap-10M Dataset

To enhance RegionBLIP's comprehension at the regional level, we propose an automated pipeline for extracting image-region-text paired data. The pipeline mainly consists of three steps: class-agnostic region discovery, regional caption generation, and regional caption refinement. Finally, we construct a dataset, dubbed RegionCap-10M, which consists of more than 5.6M images and 10.7M image-region-text paired data. The images are from the subset of CC3M, CC12M, and COYO-700M. Due to this data mining effort, our pre-trained RegionBLIP has outstanding region captioning abilities.:
